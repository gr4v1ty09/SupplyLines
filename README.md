Supply Lines is a Minecolonies/Create mod that allows players to supply their colonies with items from Create mod factories!

Instead of forcing everything into a single storage system, Supply Lines lets your colony **request items from player-built Create logistics**, making large-scale factories and rail networks feel like a natural extension of your settlement.

***

## What Does Supply Lines Do?

Supply Lines adds a **new Minecolonies building and job** that acts as a supply bridge between your colony and external production.

*   Colonists request items in their normal fashion
*   The Stock Keeper fulfills those requests if no one else in the colony can
*   Items can come from **standard storage** or **Create Stock Networks**, depending on building level
*   Couriers pick up items and deliver as usual
